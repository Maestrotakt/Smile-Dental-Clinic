Services

Braces

Braces are devices that are used to correct the alignment of teeth and bite-related problems. They consist of metal brackets, wires, and rubber bands that apply gentle pressure on the teeth to move them into the desired position.

Book an Appointent

Crown and bridges

Crown and bridges are prosthetic devices that are used to restore damaged or missing teeth. A crown is a cap that covers the entire visible part of a tooth, while a bridge is a structure that replaces one or more missing teeth by attaching artificial teeth to the adjacent natural teeth.

Book an Appointent

Whitening

Whitening is a cosmetic procedure that is used to improve the appearance of teeth by removing stains and discoloration. It can be done by applying a bleaching agent on the teeth or by using a laser or a light to activate the whitening process.

Book an Appointent

Fillings

Fillings are materials that are used to repair cavities or holes in the teeth caused by tooth decay. They can be made of metal, resin, ceramic, or glass ionomer, depending on the size and location of the cavity.

Book an Appointent

Extraction

Extraction is the removal of a tooth from its socket in the jawbone. It can be done for various reasons, such as severe decay, infection, trauma, overcrowding, or impacted wisdom teeth..

Book an Appointent

Cleaning

Cleaning is a preventive procedure that is used to remove plaque, tartar, and bacteria from the teeth and gums. It can help prevent tooth decay, gum disease, and bad breath. It usually involves scaling, polishing, and flossing the teeth.

Book an Appointent

Dental surgery

Dental surgery is a broad term that covers various types of surgical procedures that involve the teeth, gums, jaw, and facial bones. Some examples of dental surgery are dental implants, wisdom teeth removal, root canal treatment, gum grafting, and jaw reconstruction.

Book an Appointent

Dentures

Dentures are removable appliances that are used to replace missing teeth and restore the function and appearance of the mouth. They can be complete or partial, depending on the number of teeth that need to be replaced. They are made of acrylic, metal, or nylon, and are fitted to the gums by suction or clasps.

Book an Appointent

Root canal treatment

Root canal treatment is a procedure that is used to save a tooth that is infected or damaged. It involves removing the pulp, which is the soft tissue inside the tooth that contains nerves and blood vessels, and filling the root canal with a rubber-like material called gutta-percha. This prevents further infection and pain, and preserves the tooth structure.

Book an Appointent

Treatment

Treatment is a general term that refers to any type of dental service that is provided to address a specific dental problem or need. It can range from simple procedures like fillings and cleaning, to complex ones like dental surgery and orthodontics.

Book an Appointent

Implants

Implants are artificial tooth roots that are surgically placed into the jawbone to support a crown, bridge, or denture. They are made of titanium, which is biocompatible and fuses with the bone. They can provide a stable and natural-looking solution for missing teeth.

Book an Appointent

Pediatric dentistry

Pediatric dentistry is a branch of dentistry that specializes in the oral health of children from infancy to adolescence. It involves providing preventive, restorative, and educational dental services that are tailored to the specific needs and characteristics of children.

Book an Appointent

Dental x-ray

Dental x-ray is a diagnostic tool that uses low doses of radiation to produce images of the teeth and surrounding structures. It can help detect cavities, infections, bone loss, and other dental problems that are not visible to the naked eye.
