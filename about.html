Who Is NewSmile Dental Clinic?

NewSmile Dental Clinic is a modern and friendly dental practice that offers a wide range of dental services for patients of all ages. Whether you need a routine check-up, a filling, a root canal, or a cosmetic treatment, we have the expertise and equipment to provide you with the best possible care. At NewSmile Dental Clinic, we believe that oral health is an important part of your overall well-being. That's why we use the latest technology and techniques to ensure that your dental experience is comfortable and effective. We also educate our patients on how to maintain good oral hygiene and prevent dental problems in the future. Our team of dentists, hygienists, and assistants are highly qualified and experienced in their fields. They are also friendly and compassionate, and they will make you feel at ease from the moment you walk in. We are committed to providing you with personalized and professional dental care that meets your needs and expectations. If you are looking for a new smile, look no further than NewSmile Dental Clinic. We are conveniently located in Unit D Ground Floor, Hsinchu Bldg, Gov Ferrer Dr, General Trias, 4107 Cavite Philippines and we are open from Monday to Saturday, from 9 am to 5 pm. To book an appointment, please call us at 0929-718-8940 (Smart) (046) 437-1651 (Pldt) or visit our website at www.newsmile.com.ph. We look forward to seeing you soon!
